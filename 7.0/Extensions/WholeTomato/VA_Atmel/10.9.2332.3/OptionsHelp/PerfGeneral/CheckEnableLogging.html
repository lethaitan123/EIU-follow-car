Start logging to debug a problem in Visual Assist. Check only on advice from technical support at Whole Tomato Software. Setting persists only during the current session of the IDE.

Visual Assist will notify you of the location of its logs, which varies by OS, IDE, and permissions. Logs include any or all of the following: va.log, vassist.log, VaCodeInspections.log, Startup.log, and errors.log.
